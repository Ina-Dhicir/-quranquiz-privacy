Privacy Policy

Quran Quiz App  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Quran Quiz ("we", "our", or "us") is a Quran memorization quiz app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Quran Quiz is designed to work with minimal data collection. We collect the following:

  • App progress data — your completed levels, stars, and Juz progress. This is stored locally on your device only.
  • App settings — your preferences such as sound and timer settings. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase information — if you purchase Pro, Google Play processes the payment. We receive confirmation of the purchase but do not collect your payment details.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identity information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising networks or track you for advertising purposes.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use analytics tools that identify you personally.

3. Local Storage

All your progress and settings are saved locally on your device using standard browser/app storage. This data never leaves your device unless you clear your app data, in which case it is deleted permanently.

4. In-App Purchases

Quran Quiz offers an optional Pro upgrade through Google Play Billing. All payments are processed by Google Play. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment card details. By making a purchase, you agree to Google Play's Terms of Service.

5. Children's Privacy

Quran Quiz is suitable for all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. The app does not require account creation or any personal data to function.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play Services for in-app purchases. Google's privacy policy applies to their services: policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data security is managed by your device's operating system. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a passcode.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
